Through the Mechanical Engineering I get to create bamboo composite. Along with my team, we extract bamboo fibers from the culm, clean them, and prep them in a matt. We work with BASF to use sustianable copyrighted technology to spray the polypropolene. We hope to have the compsoite replace the current floor boards in a Ford Car

Why Bamboo? Bamboo is an abundant resrouce that grows extremly fast. Bamboo can grow up to three feet a day! The fibers are also very strong, they are comparable to glass fiber. What it may lack in strength it makes up in flexability. Bamboo is very flexable, which is important when it comes to cars. If a car were to crash you want the matrial in the car to not have sharp edges. Bamboo reacts by bended, but not breaking.
